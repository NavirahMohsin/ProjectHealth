Treatment:

Initial treatment for contusion should include rest, icing the affected area for 20 minutes, and applying a compression wrap to help minimize swelling. If there is an accompanying open wound, keep the area clean and bandaged. If you are uncertain about the severity of your condition or your condition does not improve with conservative treatment please contact your primary care provider or for more specialized care, contactsports medicine.

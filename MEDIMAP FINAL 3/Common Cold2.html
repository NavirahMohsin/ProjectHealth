Treatment:

To reduce the risk of spreading and getting infected with the rhinovirus, wash hands often with soap and water, avoid touching your face with unwashed hands, cover your mouth and nose when coughing and sneezing (cough into a tissue or your upper shirt sleeve), and disinfect objects and surfaces .There is no specific treatment for rhinovirus infections. Treatments include over-the-counter medicine to help with symptom relief, resting, and drinking plenty of fluids.

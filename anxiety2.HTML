Treatment:

Treatment for anxiety disorders often involves a combination of therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes. Cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) is particularly effective for many anxiety disorders, helping individuals recognize and change their thought patterns and behaviors. Medications such as selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors (SSRIs) or benzodiazepines may also be prescribed in some cases.

Top Treatment Methods used by the world:

1. Psychotherapy (e.g., cognitive-behavioral therapy)

2. Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors (SSRIs)

3. Serotonin-norepinephrine reuptake inhibitors (SNRIs)

4. Benzodiazepines (short-term use)

5. Beta-blockers (for physical symptoms)

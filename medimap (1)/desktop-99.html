Treatment:

Managing hypertension typically involves lifestyle changes

  • Healthy Diet: Eating a balanced diet low in salt, rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and low-fat dairy products (DASH diet).
  • Regular Exercise: Engaging in physical activity for at least 30 minutes most days of the week.
  • Weight Management: Maintaining a healthy weight or losing weight if overweight.
  • Limiting Alcohol and Quitting Smoking: Reducing alcohol intake and avoiding tobacco use.
  • Stress Management: Techniques like deep breathing, meditation, nd yoga can help manage stress.

Top Products used by the world:

1. ACE inhibitors (e.g., enalapril)

2. Calcium channel blockers (e.g., amlodipine)

3. Diuretics (e.g., hydrochlorothiazide)

4. Beta-blockers (e.g., metoprolol)

5. ARBs (e.g., losartan)

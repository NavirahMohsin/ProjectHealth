Treatment

  • Lifestyle Changes: Healthy diet, regular physical activity, weight management, and smoking cessation.
  • Monitoring: Regular blood sugar monitoring to manage levels effectively.
  • Medications:
  • Type 1 Diabetes: Insulin therapy (multiple daily injections or insulin pump).
  • Type 2 Diabetes: Oral medications (e.g., metformin, sulfonylureas), non-insulin injectables, and sometimes insulin.
  • Education and Support: Diabetes self-management education and support programs to help individuals manage their condition.

Top Products used by the world:

1. Metformin

2. Sulfonylureas (e.g., glibenclamide)

3. Insulin

4. DPP-4 inhibitors (e.g., sitagliptin)

5. SGLT2 inhibitors (e.g., dapagliflozin)

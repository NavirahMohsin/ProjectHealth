Contusion (bruise)

Causes and Symptoms:

  1. Causes:

Contusions are one of the most common types of injuries occurring in active children.Â A contusion, or bruise, is caused by a direct blow to the body that can cause damage to the surface of the skin and to deeper tissues as well depending on the severity of the blow.

  1. Symptoms:

Those suffering from a contusion may experience discoloration of the skin, swelling, tightness in the affected muscle, or stiffness in the affected joint. Black-and-blue marks may initially appear red or purple, and the bruised area, along with the surrounding skin, may also be tender to the touch. These symptoms indicate the presence of a contusion and should be monitored for any changes.

Pharyngitis (sore throat)

Causes and Symptoms:

  1. Causes:

Pharyngitis is caused by swelling in the back of the throat (pharynx) between the tonsils and the voice box (larynx). Most sore throats are caused by colds, the flu,virus Most cases of pharyngitis occur during the colder months. The illness often spreads among family members and close contacts. welling on the sides of your neck.

  1. Symptoms:

The main symptom is a sore throat.Other symptoms may include:fever, headache,skin rashes,joint pain and muscle ache,swollen lymph node (glands) in the neck.

Treatment:

Treatment for TB typically involves a combination of antibiotics taken for several months to effectively kill the bacteria and prevent the development of drug-resistant strains. The most common antibiotics used in TB treatment include isoniazid, rifampin, ethambutol, and pyrazinamide. Directly observed therapy (DOT) is often recommended to ensure adherence to treatment and prevent treatment failure and drug resistance.

Treatment:

While there's no cure for osteoarthritis, treatment focuses on managing symptoms and improving joint function. This may include a combination of lifestyle changes (such as weight loss and exercise), pain management (with over-the-counter or prescription medications), physical therapy, assistive devices (like braces or canes), and in severe cases, surgery (such as joint replacement). Maintaining a healthy weight, staying active with low-impact exercises like swimming or cycling, and protecting your joints from injury can all help manage osteoarthritis symptoms and slow its progression.

Top Products used by the world:

1. Acetaminophen (paracetamol)

2. NSAIDs (e.g., ibuprofen)

3. Topical creams/gels (e.g., diclofenac gel)

4. Intra-articular corticosteroid injections

5. Hyaluronic acid injections

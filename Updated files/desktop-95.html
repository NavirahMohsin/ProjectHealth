Treatment

  • Antimalarial Medications: The choice of medication depends on the species of parasite and the severity of the disease. Common treatments include artemisinin-based combination therapies (ACTs) for P. falciparum malaria and chloroquine for P. vivax malaria.
  • Severe Cases: Intravenous or intramuscular administration of artesunate or quinine is recommended for severe malaria.

Prevention

  • Mosquito Control: Use of insecticide-treated mosquito nets (ITNs), indoor residual spraying (IRS), and elimination of mosquito breeding sites.
  • Vaccine: The RTS,S/AS01 (Mosquirix) vaccine has been developed and is being used in some parts of Africa, providing partial protection against malaria in young children.

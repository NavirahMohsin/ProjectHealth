Iron Deficiency AnemiaIron

Causes and Symptoms:

  1. Causes:

Iron deficiency anemia can develop for several reasons, including: Inadequate dietary intake of iron-rich foods Poor absorption of iron from the diet (as seen in certain gastrointestinal conditions like celiac disease or inflammatory bowel disease) Blood loss (through menstruation, gastrointestinal bleeding, or other causes) Increased iron requirements during periods of rapid growth (such as infancy, adolescence, or pregnancy)

  1. Symptoms:

Symptoms of iron deficiency anemia can vary in severity but may include: Fatigue and weakness Pale skin Shortness of breath Dizziness or lightheadedness Cold hands and feet Brittle nails Headaches Rapid heartbeat (tachycardia) Chest pain, especially with exertion

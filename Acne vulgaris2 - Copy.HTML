Treatment:

Treatment for acne aims to reduce sebum production, unclog pores, reduce inflammation, and prevent scarring. Treatment options may include:

  • Topical treatments: Over-the-counter or prescription creams, gels, or lotions containing ingredients such as benzoyl peroxide, retinoids, salicylic acid, or antibiotics.
  • Oral medications: Antibiotics, oral contraceptives (for females), or isotretinoin (a potent retinoid) may be prescribed for moderate to severe acne.
  • Procedures: Dermatological procedures such as chemical peels, microdermabrasion, laser therapy, or extraction of comedones may be recommended for certain cases.
  • Lifestyle modifications: Practices such as gentle skin cleansing, avoiding excessive scrubbing or picking at acne lesions, and using non-comedogenic skincare products can help manage acne.
